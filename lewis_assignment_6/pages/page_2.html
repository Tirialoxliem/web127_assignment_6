Working Group

Let's start out with the working group. What do the working group of dogs do? These dogs are bread to do task such as pulling sleds, guarding people, helping people with needs, rescuing people, and many more things. These dogs are normally big in size due to the need for their demands. They work their jobs with absolutely no complaints. They feel honored that you trust them with your needs. Some of these breeds are Doberman Pinscher, Siberian Husky, Great Dane, Saint Bernard, Alaskan Malamute, Boxer, Rottweiler, and the Newfoundland. Do any of these dogs spike your interest?

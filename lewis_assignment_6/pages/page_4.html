Hunting and Sporting Groups

As we move on to these next two groups, you might find the need for them if you are a hunter, or someone with special needs that need help to get things for them. The next group that we are going to talk about are the hunting, and sporting groups. The hunting group are bred to run and chase for long distances in hunting. They also have a great sense of smell. The sporting group have similarities but are not bred to run and chase for very long distances. Some of the breeds in the hunting group consist of Foxhound, Beagle, Afghan Hound, Basset Hound, Greyhound, and Dachshund. As for the sporting group, they consist of Retrievers, Setters, Spaniels, Pointers, and Vizslas. Do either of these two breeds fill your needs?

Terrier Group

This next group is the terrier group. This breed was originally bred to catch rats and other rodents. With this in mind they can be a little feisty towards other animals or dogs, because they were born to get rid of other animals. Because of this they need a firm master to keep them in line. Many of these breeds are small in nature. Some of the breeds of this group consist of West Highland, Scottie, Airedale, Cairn, Border, Bull, Boston Terrier, and the Jack Russel. So, if you have the need of protection, or to get rid of pest, how do any of these breeds sound to you?
